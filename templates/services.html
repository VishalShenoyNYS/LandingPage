Counselling

Upon hearing the problems of an individual, a decision is made as to what should be done and advice could be given through the method of Directive Counseling. Non-Directive Counseling is the process of skillfully handling the problems of an individual and determining the course of action to be adopted to resolve his problem. Cooperative Counseling is the process in which both the counselor and client mutually cooperate to solve the problems of the individual.
