Training

Soft skills always give you that added edge upon your technical training that you have received as a part of your academics. Our intensive and holistic training would ensure that you develop good interpersonal and relationship-building skills which will help you to communicate and collaborate effectively at any corporate level.



Counselling

Upon hearing the problems of an individual, a decision is made as to what should be done and advice could be given through the method of Directive Counseling. Non-Directive Counseling is the process of skillfully handling the problems of an individual and determining the course of action to be adopted to resolve his problem. Cooperative Counseling is the process in which both the counselor and client mutually cooperate to solve the problems of the individual.

Coaching

Executive Coaching is all about a person working individually with an executive coach on his/her particular areas of strength and weakness to fulfil their potential. This type of coaching is a catalyst for the enhanced performance of the individual and of the teams they lead.
